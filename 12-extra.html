The <meta name="description" content=""> tag is used to provide a brief and concise description of the content of a web page. This meta tag is essential for search engine optimization (SEO) and helps search engines understand the purpose and relevance of the page.

The name="description" attribute indicates that the content inside the content attribute is a description. The content should be a summary of the page's content, typically between 150 to 160 characters. It is displayed in search engine results and can influence whether users choose to click on your page.

Including a well-crafted meta description not only improves search engine rankings but also provides users with a clear preview of what to expect when they visit your page. It's an important element for enhancing the accessibility and usability of your website.
